A paragraph is a self-contained unit of writing, a collection of sentences grouped around a single main idea or topic, used to organize text, break up long passages, and guide readers through an author's points. Key components include a topic sentence (expressing the main idea), supporting sentences (providing details, examples, or explanations), and often a concluding sentence. Paragraphs structure discourse, making longer texts more digestible and showing logical divisions in thought.
